Glossaries

This page contains some of my glossary files that you can import into toMOTko.

If you want to share your glossary files, please send them to me. I will gladly put them in this page. I plan to provide a dynamic web interface to facilitate the sharing of the glossaries but meanwhile, I do it manually.

Gacha-San, the skeleton

Contains new words from a book targeted for children where the main character is a skeleton living in a strange town of fairy beings. Very well done and funny. I liked it very much.

This glossary is in English and Japanese.

Ramu-San, the mummy

Tanoshii Ruby

This book introduces the Ruby programming language. For each chapter, I entered the words I didn't know into toMOTko. Most of the words are related to computer science but some are useful for general situations.

I've only read a few chapters so far. I will add other chapters later. It's a very slow process to read such a complicated book.

This glossary is in English and Japanese.

TanoshiiRuby.zip
Provided by Frédéric Bergeron.
